Add 2 tbsp of cofffe powder of coffe powder, add 4 tbsp of sugar to mild hot water. While taking hot water, take it in a bowl...
Step 2: Whisk until the coffe turns fluffy..
Use a hand held mixer and whisk the coffee mixture for 10 - 15 mins. Stop when the mixture turns creamy and light brown color.
Step 3: Serve it with milk....
Serve the coffe mixture with hot/cold milk. A spoon and a straw would be mandate to enjoy the coffee!!!
